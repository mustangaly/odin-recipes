White Chicken Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts or ground chicken
  • 1/2 lb ground sausage
  • 2 cans Northern white beans (do not drain)
  • 1 can corn (drained)
  • 1 can Rotel
  • 1 fresh chopped tomato
  • 1 pk McCormick white chicken chili seasoning
  • 1 pk Ranch dressing seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
  2. In a pan, cook the ground meat until browned.
  3. Combine ingredients in a Dutch Oven or large pot and bake/simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
